Common Questions

Why do I need to join a group first?

TerraMine is in closed beta, meaning only approved testers can install it from Google Play. Joining the Google Group is how Google verifies your access. Once the app launches publicly, this step won't be needed.

The Play Store says "not available" — what do I do?

It can take up to 15 minutes after joining the group for your Google account to be granted access. Try again after a few minutes, and make sure you're signed into the same Google account on both the group and your phone.

Is it safe?

Yes. TerraMine is distributed through the official Google Play Store and is free to install. No payment information is required to play.

Will I lose my progress when the full app launches?

No — your account, properties, TerraBucks, and progress are saved to the cloud. Everything carries over when the full version launches publicly.
