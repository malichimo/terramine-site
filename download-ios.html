Common Questions

Why do I need TestFlight?

Apple requires all beta apps to be distributed through TestFlight. It's a free, official Apple app — not a third-party tool. Once TerraMine launches publicly on the App Store, you'll be able to install it directly without TestFlight.

Is it safe?

Yes. TestFlight is made by Apple and is the standard way to test iOS apps before they launch. TerraMine is free and requires no payment information.

Will I lose my progress when the full app launches?

No — your account, properties, TerraBucks, and progress are all saved to the cloud. Everything carries over when the full version launches.
